NeoOptTests Eco

Welcome to NeoOptTests Eco, an open-source initiative for providing an ecosystem for analysing and search for improvements in the Neo Ecosystem.

We will start with simple statiscal analyses and move towards an interative platform for online and real-world self-adaptive parameters tuning.
